BIRTH

The Atlantic it´s a building of condominiums with 25 floors located in D street between 1st. and 3rd, in Vedado, Havana. It is located in an area privileged close to the Havana Leeve.

It is the first construction erected by the Cuban-Italian mixed enterprise Azul Inmobiliaria, founded in 1999 in association with the Italian partner B and D International and that constitutes a holding with exemption in Cuba where Punta del Morro one knows him as Investments.

Its construction began in 2000 and it was inaugurated on February 10, 2007. It is considered a symbol of the Cuban modern architecture.

STRUCTURE

It is composed by the lobby in the first floor together with an office, 5 floors of parkings, a patio and swimming pool in the sixth floor. 96 departments are distributed in 15 levels from the eighth apartment, because of 6 for plant. In the level 23 there are several swimming pools and technical places in 24 and 25. Also it is part of the set a side building of 5 floors, with three shopping centers and six housings.

MATERIALS

The project turned out to be very novel in all its aspects. Concrete cantilevers were used of high resistance and durability (up to 600 kg/cm2), cables were used also with flexible limit of 1 900 kg/mm2. The used materials were submitted to deep risk studies because the building is located in an area near to the sea and inclined to receive strong winds and penetrations of the sea when the city is flogged by hurricanes.

DESIGN

The architecture was designed by a team of Italians experts of the MSC signature, simultaneously that the structural project and for the different facility was developed by the Cuban company DCH.

FACILITIES

With a spectacular sight of the Strait of the Florida and to the city, it is a luxurious destination in the big metropolis. The rooms count with last generation services: air conditioning, lighting, fax, phones, Cable TV, video doorman, rapid elevators, safety interns, systems against intruders, emergency plant. Complemented with facility rent of cars (Havanautos), of delivery of bundles like Cubapack or DHL.
